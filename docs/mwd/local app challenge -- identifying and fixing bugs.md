^ [[local app challenge]] -- identifying and fixing bugs

---

With server-based apps you can see exceptions in your application monitoring and observability tooling.  You can also review the production data carefully to see if there are data issues.  You can often get just the right data to reproduce and fix the bug.

WIth [[design motivation -- local]] and [[design motivation -- privacy]] that can be much harder.  You won't even _know_ about an issue unless someone reports it.
