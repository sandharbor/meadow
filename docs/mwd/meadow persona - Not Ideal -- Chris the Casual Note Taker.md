^ [[meadow persona - Not Ideal]] -- Chris the Casual Note Taker

---

Chris has used Apple Notes and maybe even downloaded Obsidian at one point, but only ever created one note at a time.  His notes all go in folders and are not densely connected.

He doesn't actually want to publish his notes, but maybe hears about Meadow from a friend.  He sees all the graphs and is kinda confused about the whole thing.  If he opens the app, he won't know what is going on.

Think of showing your Neovim to your grandma (if she isn't Ada Lovelace)

We are deliberately _not_ seeking this customer, and should perhaps try to dissuade them from downloading the app and being confused by it, though we can present them with the underlying concepts.  They may be _future_ customers.

He may want to just publish a folder of not-connected notes.  Basically [[disjointed graphs]].  We'll consider that again in the future.
