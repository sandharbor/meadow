what is constitutional AI?

---

Constitutional AI refers to the idea of designing and governing artificial intelligence systems based on a set of foundational principles or rules, similar to a constitution in a legal system. These principles are intended to ensure that AI operates in a manner that is ethical, transparent, accountable, and aligned with human values and societal norms. The concept of constitutional AI is part of a broader effort to establish frameworks and guidelines for the responsible development and deployment of AI technologies.
